Pozole Rojo

Traditional Mexican stew made with meat, hominy, chile peppers, and various seasonings.

Ingredients

  • Pork
  • Water
  • Garlic
  • Seasonings
  • Tomato & Guajillo Chiles
  • Hominy
  • Toppings

Cooking Steps

  1. Boil, then simmer pork with garlic and salt until the meat is cooked enough
  2. Boil tomatoes and chiles toegether. Drain.
  3. Blend tomato and chiles with salt, garlic, oregano, cumin, and water.
  4. Shred pork, then discard bones and garlic.
  5. Stir sauce and pork into the pot, then simmer until flavors have melded.
  6. Ladle pozole into bowls and finish each serving with toppings.
  7. Enjoy with a smile!
